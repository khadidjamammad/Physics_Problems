# Section 5: Electromagnetism II - Solutions

## 1. Gauss's Law
**Problem:** A point charge of $+2 \text{ C}$ is located at the origin. Calculate the electric flux through a spherical surface of radius $1 \text{ m}$.

* **Formula:** $\Phi_E = \frac{q_{encl}}{\varepsilon_0}$
* **Calculation:** $\Phi_E = \frac{2}{8.854 \times 10^{-12}}$
* **Result:** $\Phi_E \approx 2.26 \times 10^{11} \text{ V}\cdot\text{m}$

---

## 2. Ampere's Law
**Problem:** Two parallel wires $10 \text{ cm}$ apart, currents of $5 \text{ A}$ in opposite directions. Find $B$ at the midpoint.

* **Formula:** $B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}$
* **Note:** At the midpoint ($r = 0.05 \text{ m}$), the magnetic fields from both wires point in the same direction, so they add up.
* **Calculation:** $B_{total} = 2 \times \left( \frac{4\pi \times 10^{-7} \times 5}{2\pi \times 0.05} \right)$
* **Result:** $4.0 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$

---

## 3. Biot-Savart Law
**Problem:** Wire segment $0.1 \text{ m}$, $I = 3 \text{ A}$, distance $0.2 \text{ m}$ from point $P$.

* **Formula:** $dB = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{I \cdot dl \cdot \sin\theta}{r^2}$
* **Calculation:** $B = (10^{-7}) \frac{3 \times 0.1 \times 1}{(0.2)^2}$
* **Result:** $7.5 \times 10^{-7} \text{ T}$

---

## 4. Magnetic Torque
**Problem:** Loop $10 \text{ cm} \times 5 \text{ cm}$, $I = 2 \text{ A}$, $B = 0.3 \text{ T}$ (parallel to plane).

* **Formula:** $\tau = IAB \sin\theta$
* **Calculation:** Area $A = 0.005 \text{ m}^2$, $\theta = 90^\circ$ (angle between normal and field).
* **Result:** $\tau = 2 \times 0.005 \times 0.3 = 0.003 \text{ N}\cdot\text{m}$

---

## 5. Energy Stored in a Capacitor
**Parameters:** $S = 0.02 \text{ m}^2$, $d = 0.005 \text{ m}$, $V = 500 \text{ V}$.

1.  **Capacitance:** $C = \frac{\varepsilon_0 S}{d} = \mathbf{35.4 \text{ pF}}$
2.  **Energy:** $W = \frac{1}{2}CV^2 = \mathbf{4.43 \times 10^{-6} \text{ J}}$
3.  **Electric Field:** $E = \frac{V}{d} = \mathbf{100,000 \text{ V/m}}$
4.  **Force:** $F = \frac{1}{2}QE = \mathbf{8.85 \times 10^{-4} \text{ N}}$

---

## 6. EM Wave Analysis
**Equation:** $E_y(x,t) = 100\sin(10^7x - \omega t)$

* **Direction:** $+x$ direction.
* **Wavelength:** $\lambda = \frac{2\pi}{k} = \frac{2\pi}{10^7} \approx \mathbf{6.28 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}}$
* **Angular Frequency:** $\omega = c \cdot k = (3 \times 10^8)(10^7) = \mathbf{3 \times 10^{15} \text{ rad/s}}$
* **Magnetic Field Equation:** $B_z(x,t) = 3.33 \times 10^{-7}\sin(10^7x - 3 \times 10^{15}t) \text{ T}$

---

## 7. Wavelength and Frequency
**Problem:** $550 \text{ nm}$ light.

* **Color:** Green.
* **Frequency:** $f = \frac{c}{\lambda} = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{550 \times 10^{-9}} \approx \mathbf{5.45 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}}$

## 9. Refraction (Snell's Law)
**Problem:** Air ($1.00$) to Glass ($1.50$), $\theta_1 = 30^\circ$.

* **Formula:** $n_1 \sin\theta_1 = n_2 \sin\theta_2$
* **Calculation:** $1.0 \cdot \sin(30^\circ) = 1.5 \cdot \sin\theta_2$
* **Result:** $\theta_2 = \arcsin(0.333) \approx \mathbf{19.47^\circ}$

---

## 10. Speed of Light in Media
**Problem:** Diamond ($n = 2.42$).

* **Formula:** $v = \frac{c}{n}$
* **Calculation:** $v = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{2.42}$
* **Result:** $\approx \mathbf{1.24 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}}$
