## 7. Wavelength and Frequency

**Problem Statement:** The human eye is most sensitive to light with a wavelength of about $550 \text{ nm}$. What color does this correspond to in the visible spectrum? What is the frequency of this light?

### 1. Color Identification
A wavelength of **$550 \text{ nm}$** falls directly in the middle of the visible spectrum ($380 \text{ nm}$ to $750 \text{ nm}$).
* **Color:** Green (specifically a yellowish-green).

### 2. Frequency Calculation
* **Given Wavelength ($\lambda$):** $550 \text{ nm} = 550 \times 10^{-9} \text{ m}$
* **Speed of Light ($c$):** $\approx 3 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$
* **Formula:** $f = \frac{c}{\lambda}$

**Calculation:**
$$f = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{550 \times 10^{-9}}$$
$$f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$$

### Final Answer:
The color is **Green** and the frequency is approximately **$5.45 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$**.
