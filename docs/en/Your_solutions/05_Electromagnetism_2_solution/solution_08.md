## 8. EM Spectrum

List the following types of electromagnetic radiation in order of increasing wavelength: Infrared, Ultraviolet, Microwaves, X-rays, Radio waves, Gamma rays.

### Order of Increasing Wavelength:
To list these in order of "increasing wavelength" means starting with the shortest (highest energy) and ending with the longest (lowest energy).

1.  **Gamma rays** (Shortest wavelength, highest frequency)
2.  **X-rays**
3.  **Ultraviolet**
4.  **Infrared**
5.  **Microwaves**
6.  **Radio waves** (Longest wavelength, lowest frequency)

---

### Quick Reference Table:
| Radiation Type | Wavelength Range (approx.) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Gamma rays** | $< 0.01 \text{ nm}$ |
| **X-rays** | $0.01 \text{ nm} - 10 \text{ nm}$ |
| **Ultraviolet** | $10 \text{ nm} - 400 \text{ nm}$ |
| **Infrared** | $700 \text{ nm} - 1 \text{ mm}$ |
| **Microwaves** | $1 \text{ mm} - 1 \text{ m}$ |
| **Radio waves** | $1 \text{ m} - 100 \text{ km}$ |
